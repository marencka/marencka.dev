Hi! I'm Alexis.

I'm currently a senior at UNC-Chapel Hill studying Computer Science and German.

My story started out in medicine as a biochemistry premed major. After working in the emergency department for two years, I realized that I loved science more than healthcare. Around this time, I started the process of building a gaming computer. It was here I identified my love for technology.

Despite never reading a line of code in my life, a terrible education in math, and no understanding of what computer science entailed -- I decided to take a leap of faith and switch my major to computer science. Here I found my passion for computational logic and power. I have a deep interest in machine learning, natural language processing, chaos engineering, and algorithmic efficiency.

I am currently looking for a spring/winter internship as well as a New Grad May 2022 full-time position.
